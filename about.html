INDIA

India is a diverse and culturally rich country in South Asia, known for its history, traditions, and natural beauty. It has a variety of landscapes including mountains, rivers, and beaches, attracting tourists from around the world. Famous tourist places include the iconic Taj Mahal, a symbol of love, the historic Qutub Minar, and the scenic backwaters of Kerala, making India a unique travel destination.
